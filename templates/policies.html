AI Ethics Policy

CareerMentor is built on responsible AI principles, ensuring fairness, transparency, and accountability. Our recommendations are designed to support your career growth without discrimination relating to gender, age, background, or socioeconomic status.

We rigorously test our models to reduce bias and ensure ethical decision‑making. No automated decision is made without user visibility. CareerMentor does not store sensitive personal data unless explicitly provided by the user, and even then, it is protected with strict safeguards. We are committed to building AI that supports opportunity and fairness for everyone.

Our AI systems are trained using diverse datasets to minimize structural and historical biases. We regularly audit model performance to detect unintended behavior, ensuring that the guidance provided is both relevant and inclusive for all users.

CareerMentor emphasizes explainability: users should understand why the AI makes certain recommendations. When possible, our platform provides clear reasoning or contributing factors behind suggestions, supporting transparency and user trust.

We encourage feedback from users to continue improving our AI responsibly. Any concerns regarding model behavior, fairness, or accuracy are promptly reviewed by our development team to maintain the highest ethical standards.
